    Stories are the essence of existence. What is a sentence except a tiny story? They are how we understand our experiences, create meaning, learn and bond. There are stories of all sorts here - fiction, ESL resources, audiobooks, and tales yet to be crafted. Good Studies and happy reading!

    About Author

    Rhea Black was crafting tales before her hands could write. She dictated them to her Grandma, filling volumes of notebooks. For the last 20 years, she has taught English as a Second Language, and her passion for words has inspired her throughout. Throughout that time, she has also continued to create works of fiction in all genres. Rhea currently lives in Gatineau, QC, and works in Ottawa.
