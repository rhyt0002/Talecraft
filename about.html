    • About Talecraft

    Talecraft is a platform designed to provide information about books that help ESL (English as a Second Language) students enhance their English skills. The platform focuses on books that simplify complex grammatical concepts, especially verbs, into easy-to-understand explanations and practical activities. Additionally, Talecraft provides links to where students can purchase these books, making it easier for learners to access the resources they need to improve their fluency and mastery of the English language.

    Author's Bio

    Rhea Black, an ESL professor at Algonquin College and College la Cite, is combining her 20 years of English-teaching experience and her love of writing into one project. Her textbook is entitled All Cleared Up: the Secret to Understanding Phrasal Verbs. It takes the unique approach of exploring the imaginative ways that the second word changes the meanings of verbs. For example, it explains why get up, get out, and get back are different. It also addresses why pick up can have so many different definitions. Finally, it solves the mystery of why an alarm clock can both come on and go off!

    Most learners of English as a Second Language find phrasal verbs difficult because they are so many, so varied, and so boring to learn.
