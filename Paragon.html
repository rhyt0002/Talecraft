    • Paragon

    Everybody wants something from Carol, the young, struggling ruler of Canopus. The Interplanetary Trade League wants status as a World, Out-timers want freedom from servitude to the League, and the Paragons, well, they can’t seem to agree on just what they want. Martel wants to serve Carol as a Paragon, clear the family name, and find a cure for his ailing wife. Marvarid the Eagle Huntress wants Martel as her bodyguard, and together they stumble upon a scheme that could implicate one of the Sovereign’s own elite. What the pair learns gets them captured and whisked into deep space, where the only thing Martel now wants is to get home . . . before he develops feelings for the Huntress. Buy
